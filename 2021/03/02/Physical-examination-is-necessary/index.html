Physical examination is necessary

At the beginning and the end of the year, major medical examination institutions reached the peak. As modern people pay more and more attention to health, more and more people are afraid of facing the results of the medical examination form and rejecting the medical examination for fear of finding problems. In fact, a physical examination is really necessary for maintaining health.

Through physical examination, one can understand one’s own health state and discover some early diseases that are not easy to detect, so as to prevent and stop the development of the disease in time. However, for physical examination, it’s not enough to only do examinations, they should also know how to do it.

What items should be checked in the physical examination for different age groups?

Many physical examinations are often based on a package. Both the 20-year-old boy and the 50-year-old uncle receive the same package.
Different physical examinations should be adopted for different person，and it should be choosen according to their age, gender, occupation, health status, and family medical history, etc., which is carried out from a comprehensive consideration.

How often should people carry out physical examination?

According to recommendations of the World Health Organization:
Under 45 years old, a health checkup will be carried out every one to two years;
People aged 45 and above should carry out a health check at least once a year;
People with health problems should undergo one or more physical examinations per year for corresponding health problems;
Women who have sex life should receive a gynecological examination every year.

Based on family history and personal medical history

Certain diseases with obvious family clusters, such as diabetes, stroke, coronary heart disease, breast cancer, colon cancer and so on. If there is a clear history of a certain disease in the family, physical examination items related to it should be added.

At present, scientific health management should be carried out in the medical examination industry, subsequently, the results of the physical examination should be integrated into the health management steps, so that people can enjoy a more comprehensive and systematic service, and can directly seeek medical consultation from the preventive examination when a disease problem is detected, so as to eliminate complicated processes and save more time for people.

Last but not least, health and life can’t be brought by money. As we grow old, the chances of humans suffering from certain diseases increase. Most of these diseases have no obvious symptoms in the early stage, but often have serious consequences. If you can perform regular health checks on their body, discover diseases in time, and treat them before it becomes serious, then the probability of various of diseases will be greatly reduced. A health checkup is of great significance.
