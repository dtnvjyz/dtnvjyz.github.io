Nearly perfect myopia surgery

I finally took off my eye glasses! This is the first comment I made after undergoing full femtosecond refractive surgery three years ago. Fortunately, my vision has maintained the postoperative level for three years without any adverse reactions.

Before deciding to undergo refractive surgery, my father and I compared differences of various of surgical options, and also saw many postoperative cases on the Internet. At that time, I wanted to remove the glasses, but I could only see the experience sharing of “success” and “no regrets”. I think those who have “sequelae” are unlucky, which I will definitely not encounter.

However, in retrospect, everyone did mention a lot of valuable information, but I selectively ignored it. Based on my experience, I still think that myopia laser surgery is very close to perfect in existing surgeries.

When it comes to the operation process, there is probably a negative pressure ring that fixes the eyes to prevent the eyeballs from turning randomly, and then the button is activated，after the doctor operates for tens of seconds, and the operation will be over，which is easy and efficient. After I successfully removed the eye glasses, many of my friends wanted to try it, either, but not everyone can do myopia surgery.

No surgery is 100% safe，there may also be many contraindications, such as some diseases related to the eye and systemic diseases. Whether the surgery can be carried out or what kind of surgery can be carried out depends on the preoperative examination and the doctor’s judgment. For example, my roommate could not accept the operation because of trichiasis, then why I say that this operation is almost perfect?

Laser surgery can meet the needs of most people

Simply speaking, laser myopia surgery can thin the cornea. The higher the myopia degree, the thicker the cornea that needs to be cut. In terms of surgery method, a principle that suits most people is:

  1. When there is sufficient corneal thickness, the degree and other conditions are also suitable, half femtosecond or full femtosecond is preferred
  2. If the corneal thickness is insufficient and the patient is of moderate to low level of myopia, then surface surgery such as t-PRK can be given priority.
  3. In recent years, lens implantation (ICL) has gradually become mainstream.

Safe operation process

Laser surgery for myopia is a highly programmed ultra-fast operation. Unexpected results rarely occur during and after the operation, and there is almost no opportunity for doctors to make mistakes.

Less adverse reactions after the surgery

With technological innovation, the incidence of many common postoperative complications (dry eye syndrome, poor wound healing) has also been greatly reduced. But after experiencing many “unsatisfied” cases, I hope that everyone will weigh this operation in a comprehensive way. A reasonable psychological expectation is a necessary prerequisite.

Im brief, myopia surgery is a choice after fully weighing risks and benefits，hope that the development of science and technology will bring us closer and closer to a safer and perfect operation.
