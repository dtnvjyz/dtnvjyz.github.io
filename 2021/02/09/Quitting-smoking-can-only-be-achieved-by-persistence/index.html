Quitting smoking can only be achieved by persistence

When it comes to smoking and the prevention of lung cancer, I always think of a terrifying experience. Once upon a time, I went to the pathology building of the Third Hospital of Peking University to deliver something, but happened to meet them sorting out some specimens. Many lung specimen of patients who had been smoking for decades and those diagnosed with lung cancer appeared in front of me, i suddenly felt a little scared and nauseous.Since then, I have made up my mind to discourage my boyfriend from quitting smoking, but after a few years, it still didn’t work. It seems that quitting smoking is a long battle, and only through persistence can people quit smoking.

Lung cancer is the largest cancer in the world

Let me first talk about why governments all over the world are appealing to quit smoking. After all, as the world’s second largest cancer, lung cancer has led to an increasing number of people to die in the world, which is still increasing year by year. Furthermore, Lung cancer has become the cancer with the highest age-standardized incidence rate (ASIR) and age-standardized mortality rate (ASMR) in the world.

Tobacco control is the most important measure in preventing lung cancer

As is known to all, smoking is the most important factor causing lung cancer, and most of the incidence and death of lung cancer can be attributed to smoking. China is the world’s largest tobacco producer and consumer, and there are more than 300 million smokers in China, 740 million people are harmed by second-hand smoke, and more than 1.2 million people die from smoking and second-hand smoke every year.

Faced with such terrifying numbers, I hope that when China vigorously calls for quitting smoking, it is like some countries that it can also forcefully require pictures of smoking hazards to be printed on the packaging to give people a warning effect. Regardless of the country, tobacco control is the most important factor in the prevention and control of lung cancer.

Quitting smoking is definitely good, but simultaneously, it is really difficult

Five tips for quitting smoking:

  1. Always remind yourself that smoking is a disease
  2. Getting rid of nicotine should be compulsive
  3. Remember that smoking affects everything about you
  4. Make up your mind before you quit smoking
  5. Seek help from a doctor or psychologist

Based on the experience of my friends, it is very difficult to quit smoking, and a little temptation can easily cause quitters to smoke again. Simultaneously, related studies have shown that quitters will experience weight rebound, cardiovascular disease and increased risk of diabetes in the early stages of quitting smoking. Smokers have to quit smoking for more than 15 years before their lung function recover as a non-smoker. From a long-term perspective, there is no doubt that quitting smoking is beneficial for human health!

Even the soldiers in WeChat emojis have successfully quit smoking, don’t wait, for the sake of health, quit smoking right now!

To toast, our health!
